American odds are the format typically used by sports books and casinos in the United States.Â  Favorite sides are represented by negative numbers and underdog sides by positive numbers.Â  Negative American odds can be thought of as “wager X credits to win 100 credits” where X is the odds number appearing after the minus sign.Â  Positive American odds can be thought of as “wager 100 credits to win X credits” where X is the odds number appearing after the plus sign.

Spread Bet

A spread bet is a bet in which two teams or sides are considered to be unequal and an adjustment will be made to the number of points scored by that team so that the two outcomes are equally probable and can be offered at or near even odds.Â  Teams or sides that are favorites will have points subtracted from their score, while teams or sides that are underdogs will have points added.Â  The number of points that will be subtracted or added is called the “spread” or the “line”.
