What is Lose the Juice and Why Play?

Lose the Juice is the first of its kind. It’s the only free sports app that lets you compete against your friends and neighbors for prizes from participating local venues like bars, restaurants, and breweries.

You simply make picks based on money lines, point spreads, and point totals for your favorite sporting events on the Lose the Juice app.  It’s free to play and the winners can receive prizes from participating venues.
